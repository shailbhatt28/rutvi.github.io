About Rutvi

This is Rutvi. Rutvi is an absolute legend. Be like Rutvi.

 

Born on the 29th of August, 1999 (one day after another legend was born), Rutvi Shalin Sheth, legally named Chitra, has now completed 19 years. With an unbelievable life that includes having met Rajnikanth, gotten into a fight with the police, topping her university, beating up friends' abusive exes, drawing book covers, making international business deals, and getting a solid boyfriend (lol), Rutvi's journey has been tremendous.

She dances, she paints, she acts, she reached district levels in badminton, she broke her leg and still won the relay race, she teaches, she clicks beautiful photographs, she cooks, the list goes on. But most of all, she lives, and she makes every single day worthwile.
